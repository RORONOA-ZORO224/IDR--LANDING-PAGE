About IDR

Academic Insight.
Industry Precision.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute operating at the intersection of academic insight and live industry application. Through strategic partnerships with leading UK universities, IDR develops cohorts of rigorously trained digital, cyber, and AI risk practitioners — deployment-ready from day one.

We operate exclusively in regulated, high-consequence environments: financial services, critical national infrastructure, and the public sector. Our model bridges the gap between theoretical frameworks and real-world risk — ensuring every practitioner we develop is calibrated to the complexity of modern threat landscapes and evolving governance obligations.
